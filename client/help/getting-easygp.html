Getting EasyGP

At the present time, you can only install a development version of EasyGP and only in a development environment of gambas 3.0 which may not be available as a package in your particular distribution.

Mimumum Requirements are

  • Linux - a distribution of your choice and a working knowledge of linux
  • Postgresql - a working version of at least 8.3 or greater
  • Gambas3 - development version installed on your distribution.
As there are many different ways to get this all working, if you encounter problems, please send your questions to the the EasyGP developers list.
